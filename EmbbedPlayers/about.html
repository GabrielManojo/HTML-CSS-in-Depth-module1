About Us

Little Lemon is a family-owned restaurant serving the best Mediterranean cuisine in town! We are passionate about using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to create delicious and authentic dishes that our customers love. Our menu features a variety of options, including vegetarian and gluten-free choices, to cater to all dietary preferences. We pride ourselves on providing excellent customer service and a warm, welcoming atmosphere for all our guests.
